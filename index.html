General health risk assessment scale

Score of Health effects outcomes.

0 < Index <1. Performance preservation. Human / employee performance is not compromised by environmental factors.

1 <= Index <3. Risk of performance impairment (presenteeism) due to indoor environment factors, there is a risk of reduced employee performance / productivity.

3 <= Index <5. Risk of short-term labour incapacity 2-3 days. Due to indoor environment factors, there is a risk of short disability, which will recover within 1-3 days. During this period, the employee cannot perform his duties and be absent from work to restore his ability to work

5 <= Index <7. Risk of medium -term labour incapacity (more than 5 days).

7 <= Index <9. Risk of admission. Due to indoor environment there is a risk of admission, a health condition that requires a person to be hospitalized for hospital care

Index >=9+. Risk of professional labour disability. Occupational disability risk means that the person will be unable to continue their professional activities.

X

Productivity value is measured as a percentage. For example, 95 means a potential loss of productivity - 5%
